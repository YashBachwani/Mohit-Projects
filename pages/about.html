About Mohit

Mohit Jagwani is an entrepreneur, life coach and one of India‘s leading speakers.
Mohit‘s goal is to help people be the most confident and powerful version of themselves through his unique ability to speak with clarity and impact.
The way Mohit does this is through conducting masterclasses, giving keynotes, creating content and providing programs to people that would like to avail his services.

As a life coach, Mohit works with people along 5 pillars of life which are
1. Mindset
2. Fitness
3. Communication
4. Focus
5. Money

When it comes to Mindset, Mohit has helped people get out of depression and build a strong mindset to take on the challenges of life with a smile.
In terms of fitness, Mohit is a hybrid calisthenics athlete with immense strength and helps people
understand the mind body connection and how they can utilize working out to improve their
focus, mental strength and overall well being.

Mohit helps people become confident, powerful and charismatic communicators through his storytelling, public speaking and communication sessions.

Focus is a super power especially in today‘s day and age, Mohit helps people achieve this super power and improve it by helping people
align their work with their inner core values and life purpose. Leveraging the neuroscience
of flow and various other time management and productivity principles to help people holistically
approach their work and life getting the maximum out of both of them.

In terms of money, Mohit helps people realise their true strengths and how they can utlize their true strengths to make money while
living a life that is aligned with their purpose.

Mohit has headlined events with organisations like Porsche, IIM Ahmedabad, LD Engineering College and many more esteemed organisations
with this teachings reaching hundreds and thousands of people.

So whether you want to become an exceptional public speaker and communicator, gain clarity about your career and life path
or just want to become a more confident and powerful version of yourself.

Mohit is one of the best there is.
